What are Puffbums?

Small enough to keep close. Soft enough to reach for often.

Puffbums are soft tactile companions made for moments when you want to pause, breathe, and feel a little more grounded.

They are not complicated wellness tools. They are simple, squeezable anchors that make breathing feel more physical, more comforting, and easier to return to.

Tactile-led breathing

Let your hands guide your breath.

Instead of thinking your way into calm, Puffbums gives your body something soft and steady to follow.

1

Place Oceanbum in your palm and notice its softness, shape, and weight.

Why touch helps

A physical cue for a busy mind.

Slow breathing can support a calmer state, while touch gives the brain a clear sensory point to focus on. Puffbums combines both: a soft squeeze to anchor attention and a longer exhale to help you settle. It is everyday support, not a medical device or treatment.

FAQ

Questions before you buy?

Here are the essentials about Puffbums, Oceanbum, PuffZenDen access, and the difference between the bundle and replacement product.

Is Puffbums the same as PuffZenDen?

No. Puffbums is the physical tactile companion product line. PuffZenDen is the guided calm app that works with Puffbums.

What is included in the Oceanbum Founder Bundle?

The Founder Bundle includes the Oceanbum tactile companion and 1 year of PuffZenDen calm access. Your app activation code is sent to your email after purchase.

What is the Oceanbum replacement product?

The replacement Oceanbum is the physical Puffbum only. It does not include PuffZenDen app activation access.

Do I need the app to use Oceanbum?

No. You can hold, squeeze, and breathe with Oceanbum on its own. The app adds guided breathing sessions, dens, affirmations, sounds, and calm rituals.

Is Puffbums a medical product?

No. Puffbums is designed for everyday comfort, grounding, and calm routines. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition.
